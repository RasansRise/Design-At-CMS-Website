Vision, Mission, Aspirations and Experience

Mission

Our mission is to empower brands to connect with their audience through exceptional exhibit designs that capture attention and drive engagement. With a focus on creativity, quality, and attention to detail, we provide end-to-end solutions – from design concepts and visual media to digital marketing – that align with each client’s unique vision and objectives. We are committed to building lasting client relationships based on trust, transparency, and collaboration, ensuring that every project not only meets but exceeds expectations.

Vision

Our vision at DesignatCMS is to set a global standard for innovative and impactful trade show exhibit design. We aspire to be the go-to partner for brands seeking to make unforgettable impressions, creating exhibits that blend creativity, precision, and engagement across both physical and virtual platforms. We envision a future where every brand can transform their story into a compelling, immersive experience that resonates with audiences worldwide.

Experience

With over 23 years of specialized experience, DesignatCMS (CMS) has developed a reputation for excellence in trade show exhibit design. Over the years, we’ve worked with a diverse range of clients across industries and borders, bringing bold ideas and refined strategies to every project.From immersive 3D renderings to meticulously detailed shop drawings, our design approach is rooted in both creativity and precision, ensuring that each exhibit meets the highest standards of quality and impact. Our longstanding industry presence equips us with a unique understanding of the evolving needs and challenges in trade show design, allowing us to deliver solutions that resonate and leave a lasting impression.

Aspirations

At DesignatCMS, we aspire to continually push the boundaries of exhibit design, incorporating the latest technologies and innovative approaches to elevate brand experiences. Our goal is to expand our reach, creating lasting partnerships with clients across the globe and becoming a trusted resource for all their trade show design needs. We aim to inspire, engage, and leave a lasting impact on every audience we reach
