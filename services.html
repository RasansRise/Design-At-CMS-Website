Exclusive Services

At DesignatCMS, we offer a full suite of services designed to elevate your trade show exhibit and brand presence. From impactful design to strategic digital marketing, our services help you create a memorable experience that resonates with your audience during and beyond the event.

Exhibit Trade Show Booth Design

Our custom exhibit designs bring your brand to life with precision and creativity. We offer:

  • 3D Visual Design : Using advanced 3D software, we create lifelike renderings that provide you with clear, detailed visuals of your booth from every angle. Our 3D design package includes both high-quality drawings and an immersive video walkthrough, explaining every corner of your exhibit and fully expressing the design concept. This helps you envision the final look and feel of your booth, ensuring it aligns perfectly with your brand identity and event goals.

  • Shop Drawings : Our precise shop drawings provide detailed specifications for booth construction, ensuring seamless production and setup to bring your vision to reality.

Branding

Effective branding is essential for making a lasting impact. Our branding services include developing a cohesive brand identity that flows across all aspects of your exhibit. From logo creation and color schemes to messaging and visual style, we create a consistent, strong brand presence that resonates with your audience and enhances brand recognition.

Virtual Solutions

Our virtual solutions extend the reach of your exhibit to remote audiences, creating interactive, immersive experiences online. This enables you to engage with visitors worldwide, broadening your audience and keeping your exhibit accessible long after the event concludes.

Physical Engagement Media

We specialize in interactive, hands-on media that enhances visitor engagement. Our physical engagement options include interactive touchscreens, product displays, and motion-sensor installations, creating memorable experiences and turning passive attendees into active participants.

Advertising Material Design

We create a full range of advertising materials to support your trade show presence. From printed collateral like brochures, banners, and signage to digital assets such as e-flyers, we ensure every piece aligns with your brand messaging and contributes to a cohesive presence across all channels.

Sponsored Ads

Our sponsored ads service maximizes your reach and visibility, attracting attendees to your booth before and during the event. We develop targeted ad campaigns across platforms like Google, LinkedIn, and social media to increase brand awareness and drive qualified traffic to your exhibit.
