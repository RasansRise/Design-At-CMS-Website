Our Proven Design Process

Our process

At DesignatCMS, we believe that a well-defined process leads to exceptional results. Here’s a step-by-step look at how we bring your vision to life:

Prev
Next

Discovery & Consultation

We begin by understanding your brand, objectives, and audience. Through detailed brief, we gather insights that inform the design direction.

Concept Development

Our creative team transforms your goals into initial concepts, exploring layout, branding, and engagement ideas. We work closely with you to ensure our ideas align with your vision.

3D Visual Design & Shop Drawings

Using state-of-the-art software, we produce detailed 3D renderings and shop drawings. These provide a realistic preview of your booth and the technical specs needed for a flawless build.

Media & Marketing Integration

We develop any additional media elements – from interactive touchpoints to video displays – ensuring a cohesive experience that captivates your audience.

Execution & Production

For physical setups, we coordinate with production teams in select regions to execute the design with precision. Our virtual solutions are similarly crafted for smooth implementation.

Post-Event Analysis & Support

After the event, we review the exhibit’s performance and gather feedback, helping you plan for future success.
